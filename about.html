About us

At Dua Knitting, we specialize in high-quality knitting services using circular knitting machines. With years of expertise in the textile industry, we provide premium knitted fabrics tailored to meet diverse market demands. Our state-of-the-art facility ensures precision, efficiency, and durability in every fabric we produce, catering to both small and large-scale orders. In addition to circular knitting, we also offer manual Jacquard fabric production, bringing craftsmanship and intricate designs to life. Our skilled team is dedicated to delivering superior-quality fabrics with unique textures and patterns, meeting the needs of fashion, home textiles, and industrial applications. At Dua Knitting, we combine innovation with tradition to create fabrics that stand out in quality and design.
